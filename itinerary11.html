SOUTHEAST ASIAN MARKET

area: fdr park

cost: $5-$20 per person

cuisine: khmer, laotian, malaysian, vietnamese (+ more)

FDR Park has historically been a community space for the refugees of Cambodia and Laos. Initially a secret market, facing discrimination and police scrutiny, over the years it has grown into an important staple of Philadelphia's history of immigrants.
