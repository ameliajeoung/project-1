ABOUT

I moved to Philadelphia at 8 years old, essentially spending the majority of my formative adolescence there.

I always knew I wanted to go out-of-state for college, but after my freshman year I was so happy to spend a few months at home. I've always loved it, but I gained a newfound appreciation for Philly after time away.

More and more I find myself centering art projects around Philly.
